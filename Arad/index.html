Arad Font

Persian-Arabic font

Download

Version 1.0.1

Arad is an open source font, which is a continuation of the Shabnam font project. Arad is designed in 8 weights and three-dots style, and according to the structure and multiple weights, it is suitable for texts and stands out in headlines. Kanit font is used for English and small changes have been made in its structure.
